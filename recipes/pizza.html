Pizza Recipe

Description

Above is an image of a fully cooked BBQ Chicken Pizza which includes a thin crust, diced chicken, cooked vegetables, and BBQ sauce.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Diced Chicken
  • Any Selection of Vegetables
  • BBQ Sauce
  • Mozzerella Cheese

Steps

  1. Prepare the Pizza Dough
  2. Preheat the Oven
  3. Prepare the vegatables (Chop)
  4. Roll out the Pizza Dough
  5. Shape the Pizza Dough
  6. Apply the BBQ Sauce
  7. Put the toppings on the pizza (Vegetables and Diced Chicken)
  8. Add the Cheese
  9. Put the pizza in the oven and watch the pizza carefully
  10. Take the pizza out of the oven once it is ready
