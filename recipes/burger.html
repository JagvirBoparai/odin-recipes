Fried Chicken Burger Recipe

Description

Above is an image of a fully cooked Fried Chicken Burger that includes lettuce, cheddar cheese, a fried chicken patty, and regular buns, with mayonnaisse.

Ingredients

  • Regular Buns
  • Fried Chicken Patty
  • Lettuce + Any other Vegetables of your choosing
  • Mayonnaise + Any other Sauces of your choosing
  • Cheddar Cheese Slice

Steps

  1. Heat up your Fried Chicken Patty using a regular pan
  2. Toast your regular buns
  3. Prepare the vegatables to be placed in the burger
  4. Once your regular buns are toasted, apply mayonnaise + any sauce to both of them
  5. Place lettuce and other vegetables on the bottom bun
  6. Once your Fried Chicken Patty is ready, place on top of the vegetables
  7. Place the cheddar cheese slice on top of the Fried Chicken Patty
  8. Finish the burger by closing it with the top bun.
