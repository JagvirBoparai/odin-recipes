Steak Recipe

Description

Above is an image of a pan seared steak cooked to a tempurature of medium with butter

Ingredients

  • Raw Steak
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Any additional seasoning of your choice

Steps

  1. Preheat the pan on the stovetop
  2. Apply salt+pepper to your steak (make sure to use a considerable amount of seasoning)
  3. Add any additional seasoning of your choosing
  4. Once the pan is hot, add some butter to the pan
  5. Place the steak in the pan
  6. Keep monitoring the steak as it cooks to ensure the temperature inside is good and it is seared properly on the outside
  7. Note: Flip the steak within the pan as needed and keep monitoring
  8. Once the steak is cooked to your preferred tempurature, take it out of the pan and let the steak rest before eating.
